1. Purpose of This Agreement

This document serves as formal confirmation that the Client understands and agrees to proceed with Connected Education's admission and visa support services, despite having a complex or high-risk profile that may impact the outcome of their application.

2. Definition of Complex Profile

For the purposes of this Agreement, a "complex profile" includes, but is not limited to, the following situations:

  • Client's academic grade average is below 70%, or they have any subject grades below "C."
  • Client fails to provide required or sufficient documents for their admission application.
  • Client willingly takes risks against the Service Provider's recommendations or refuses to take necessary steps to meet admission requirements.
  • Client is offered limited institution or program options due to a below-average academic profile (e.g., grades below 70% or "C," IELTS below 6.0 overall or 5.5 in each band, no GMAT/GRE scores, etc.).
  • Client has an educational gap exceeding three (3) years for an intended Bachelor's degree or five (5) years for an intended Master's degree.
  • Client is aged over 28 years at the time of application.
  • Client is applying to a high visa refusal rate country based on verified online sources.
3. Client Acknowledgement

By signing this Agreement, the Client confirms that:

  • They have been clearly informed of the risks associated with their profile.
  • They understand that Connected Education cannot guarantee admission, offer letter, or visa approval.
  • They acknowledge that final decisions rest solely with the relevant educational institution or immigration authority.
4. Acceptance of Risk and No Refund Policy
  • The Client voluntarily agrees to proceed with Connected Education's services despite the known risks described above.
  • The Client fully accepts that all service fees paid are non-refundable, regardless of admission or visa outcome.
  • This policy applies in all cases, including but not limited to:
    • Admission or visa refusal.
    • Offer letter delay or withdrawal.
5. Service Provider's Commitment

Connected Education agrees to:

  • Provide professional support and guidance throughout the admission and visa process.
  • Communicate updates, requirements, and recommendations to the Client transparently and promptly.

However, Connected Education shall not be held liable for any outcome beyond its control, including but not limited to rejection, delay, or regulatory changes.
